About us

Knits N Knots is a textile business located in Surat, Gujarat. We specialize in producing high-quality fabrics. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best quality fabrics at competitive prices. We have a wide range of fabrics, from cotton to Viscose, and we also offer custom-made designs. Every product is made with care and attention to detail. Our team of experienced craftsmen works hard to ensure the highest quality of our products. We are proud to provide our customers with products that are crafted with attention to detail and craftsmanship.We are constantly expanding and innovating our products to meet the ever-changing demands of our customers. Our products are made to last and we always strive to make sure our customers are satisfied with their purchase. We take great care to ensure that our products are of the highest quality and that they meet all the necessary standards. We are proud to be a part of the textile industry in Surat and we look forward to continuing to provide our customers with the highest quality products.
